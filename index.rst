Our Goal
--------

Our goal for these docs are to be a the #1 community resource for the :ref:`YOMIH <yverb-yomih>` Modding community go to find quick information about modding, without having to search the depths of `Discord <https://discord.gg/yomimodding>`_ and `Reddit <https://www.reddit.com/r/yomimodding>`_ for *answers* .
*(Almost like a homepage of sorts)*

Now we don't want to steal the thunder from the actual :ref:`Community Channels <ydoc-community-channels>`, so please go visit them!
